We empower students to explore and solve meaningful problems with technology.

We're hackers in the purest form: We don't let anything get in our way; We tinker and play; We find clever solutions to hard problems; We use technology to get more done faster, elegantly; We are prolific connectors across different disciplines; We learn from and teach each other; We may program applications, design interfaces, write articles, direct movies, build bridges, or start companies; We <3 GIFs.

— Tech@NYU Leadership

Initiatives

Students, feel free to attend any of our initiatives!

  • DemoDays

    Tech@NYU is proud to announce DemoDays–a monthly meetup where students can share what they've been working on with other students. Make friends with hackers, designers, entrepreneurs, and other generally awesome people from all around NYC!

    Once a month on Saturday, evening

    Celebrate with us at the next DemoDays →

  • HackDays

    HackDays are 4-6 hour mini hackfests. If you’ve never coded anything before, HackDays is a great place to learn. If you’re a developer, it’s a nice place to be productive or try a new framework.

    Once a week on Friday, late afternoon to early evening

    Hang out with us, learn, and code at the next HackDays →

  • DesignDays

    Design solutions to any problems.

    Once every two weeks on Tuesday evening

    Talk design with us at the next DesignDays →

  • Startup Week

    This epic, flagship event kicks off with the hackNY hackathon, followed by a week of talks and discussion by the country's top innovators, investors, engineers, and designers. A great way to be introduced to new perspectives.

    Once a semester

    See a promotional video →

  • GameDays

    NYC has a thriving game development industry.

    Once every two weeks

    Attend a workshop →

  • @ Hours

    Connect one-on-one with the top technologists in NYC, to hear their story, get advice, and have great conversation.

    Once every Friday, noon

    Who has set aside time to meet next? →

  • GoodDays

    Do good!

    Once every two weeks, in the evening

    Do good with us! →
