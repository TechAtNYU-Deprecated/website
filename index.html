Tech@NYU Digest

Our email digest, sent to 4,500 subscribers every two weeks, keeps NYC updated on events for students and technologists.

We bring together passionate students who realize the potential of technology.

We help them ask questions and realize visions through design, programming, connections, and by providing a venue to show off their work and inspire others.

Initiatives

Students, feel free to attend any of our initiatives. We have no formal membership, so if you consider yourself part of the Tech@NYU community, consider yourself a member! PS. Startup Week is open to the public!

  • DemoDays

    Tech@NYU is proud to announce DemoDays–a monthly meetup where students can share what they've been working on with other students. Make friends with hackers, designers, entrepreneurs, and other generally awesome people from all around NYC!

    Once a month on Saturday, evening

    Celebrate with us at the next DemoDays →

  • HackDays

    HackDays are 4-6 hour mini hackfests. If you’ve never coded anything before, HackDays is a great place to learn. If you’re a developer, it’s a nice place to be productive or try a new framework.

    Once a week on Friday, late afternoon to early evening

    Hang out with us, learn, and code at the next HackDays →

  • DesignDays

    Design solutions to any problems.

    Once every two weeks on Tuesday evening

    Talk design with us at the next DesignDays →
