Tech@NYU

We bring together passionate students who tinker, play, build elegant things—and ship them.

We help them learn more about design, technology, and startup culture, and give them a venue to show off their work.

  • HackDays

    HackDays are 4-6 hour mini hackfests. If you’ve never coded anything before, HackDays is a great place to learn. If you’re a developer, it’s a nice place to be productive or try a new framework.

  • DemoDays

    Tech@NYU is proud to announce DemoDays–a monthly meetup for student hackers, designers, entrepreneurs, and other generally awesome human people from all around NYC!

  • DesignDays

    Design solutions to any problem

  • Startup Week

    A week long of hacking, networking, and learning alongside the best and brightest in NYC tech.
