Tech@NYU

Tech@NYU is empowering students to explore and solve problems with technology.

  • HackDays

    HackDays are 4-6 hour mini hackfests. If you’ve never coded anything before, HackDays is a great place to learn. If you’re a developer, it’s a nice place to be productive or try a new framework.

  • DemoDays

    Tech@NYU is proud to announce DemoDays–a monthly meetup for student hackers, designers, entrepreneurs, and other generally awesome human people from all around NYC!
