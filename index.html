We empower students to explore and solve meaningful problems with technology.

We are hackers in the purest form: We don't let anything get in our way; We tinker and play; We find clever solutions to hard problems; We use technology to get more done faster, elegantly; We are prolific connectors across different disciplines; We learn from and teach each other; We may program applications, design interfaces, write articles, direct movies, build bridges, or start companies; We <3 GIFs.

— Tech@NYU Leadership

Initiatives

Students, feel free to attend any of our initiatives!

  • DemoDays

    A monthly meetup where we demo what we've been working on. Make friends with other hackers, designers, entrepreneurs, and other generally awesome people from all around NYC!

    Celebrate with us at the next DemoDays →

  • HackDays

    HackDays are social coding sessions with talks from software engineers, for either complete beginners and advanced developers alike.

    Hang out with us, learn, and code at the next HackDays →

  • DesignDays

    We have partnered with our friends at Parsons design school to learn and share design knowledge. We cover the fundamentals of design through short expert talks and interactive design exercises.

    Talk design with us at the next DesignDays →

  • Startup Week

    This epic, flagship event kicks off with the hackNY hackathon, followed by a week of talks and discussion by the country's top innovators, investors, engineers, and designers. A great introduction to the NYC technology scene.

    See a promotional video →

  • GameDays

    NYC has a thriving game development industry. This initiative teaches themes ranging from technical game mechanics and the computational problems involved, to conceptual design involving narrative, music, and art.

    Attend the next workshop →

  • Freshman Circuit

    The Freshman Circuit introduces 10-15 freshmen students to the thriving technology culture in NYC. This program will incorporate Tech@NYU events, group tours to prominent NYC startups and tech companies, as well as informal group excursions.

    Interested? Contact Abhi for details.
